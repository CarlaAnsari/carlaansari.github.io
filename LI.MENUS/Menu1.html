The Galeria Moda is an institution celebrating the talents of the most important photographers since the 1950s. The artists represented by Galeria Moda are worldwide well-known photographers and have worked under the name of the biggest press magazines : Vogue, Harper’s Bazaar, Vanity Fair, Elle…
